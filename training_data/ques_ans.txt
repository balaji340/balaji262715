What is the difference between analog and digital signals?
Analog signals represent data in a continuous wave, while digital signals use discrete values, typically 0s and 1s, to represent data.
What does CPU stand for in computers?
CPU stands for Central Processing Unit, which is the brain of the computer, responsible for executing instructions.
What is an LED?
LED stands for Light Emitting Diode. It's a semiconductor device that emits light when current flows through it.
How can I extend my smartphone battery life?
You can extend battery life by lowering screen brightness, turning off unused apps, and enabling power-saving modes.
What does mAh mean in a battery?
mAh stands for milliampere-hour and is a unit used to measure a battery's capacity. A higher mAh value generally means the battery can last longer.
Why does my device overheat when charging?
Overheating can happen if the charging cable or adapter is damaged, the device is running multiple heavy applications, or the device is in a hot environment. Try charging in a cooler, well-ventilated space.
